About Me

I'm a Robotics Engineer and Software Developer with extensive experience in developing cutting-edge robotic systems. Currently serving as a Software Engineering Coordinator at LA2 Dynamics, I focus on building and enhancing software for quadruped robots.

As Team Captain of the ITU AUV Team, I've led our team to international success, securing first place at the Singapore AUV Challenge and second place at the European Robotics League RAMI Competition.

I also run my own business, Poly Design 3D, where I develop innovative plugins for 3D modeling applications, enhancing the workflow of artists and designers worldwide.
