About Me

I'm a Robotics Engineer and Software Developer driven by the challenge of creating intelligent, autonomous systems. My focus is on building robust software and integrating complex hardware to bring advanced robotic capabilities to life.

Currently, at LA2 Dynamics, I coordinate software development efforts, concentrating on enhancing the locomotion, perception, and overall intelligence of quadruped robots through well-architected software solutions.

Previously, leading the ITU AUV Team provided invaluable experience in managing technical projects and fostering collaboration, culminating in international competition victories in the demanding field of underwater robotics.

Complementing this, I founded Poly Design 3D, where I develop practical software tools that bridge engineering principles with the creative needs of 3D artists worldwide.
